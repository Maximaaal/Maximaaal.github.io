Best Before Expired

Generative Art

What is someone's relationship towards digital photos compared to analog photos? This research question has evolved into creating Best Before Expired: a tool that grows generated digital mold on photos. The mold grows on the photos based on their last opened date. If there was such a thing as digital mold, would their value of digital files change?
