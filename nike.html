Nike Kid's Pod

Interactive Installation

Your body as a controller in Nike's House of Innovation Paris

The Kid's Pod is a game station with a difference: you are the controller and there are no rules. The movements of your body shape and change the journey of the on-screen athletes, challenging them to excel and activate three different 'innovations'. There's no beginning and no end: all you need to do is step in front of the screen and explore. Inspired by the spontaneity and energy of parkour, the possibilities of the Kid's Pod are limitless. With no instructions or interface, the hexagonal structure creates a 360Â° play area that invites kids to discover the game intuitively, and even team up for a multiplayer mode. The more you play, the more you learn.
