ABOUT

My name is Max Rentmeester, a graphic and interactive designer born and raised in Eindhoven, currently based in Rotterdam. The main focus of my work is to create experiences between user and technological interaction, while being mindful of the implications of using certain techniques. These techniques include (creative) programming, web design, 3D rendering, and graphic design.
